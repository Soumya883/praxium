# V2 Roadmap - Praxium ERP

This document outlines planned premium add-on modules and integration ideas to guide future sales discussions and development priorities. The core platform is fully stabilized; these features will be validated through paid customer commitments before implementation.

---

## 🤖 1. The AI "Doubt Solver" (Premium Add-On)
A student-facing chatbot integrated into the Student Portal allowing student users to get immediate step-by-step assistance outside of classroom hours.

* **Target Pricing Model**: Premium add-on charging institutes an additional **₹2,000/month**.
* **Key Features**:
  * **Image-to-Solution OCR**: Students upload a photo of a math, physics, or chemistry problem.
  * **Step-by-Step AI Tutor**: Uses multimodal models (e.g. Gemini 1.5 Pro / GPT-4o) to break down the solution step-by-step with explanations rather than just showing the answer.
  * **Physics/Math Formula Rendering**: Rich LaTeX formatting for mathematical expressions.
  * **Teacher Review Queue**: A dashboard for teachers to see what doubts were solved and flag incorrect/suboptimal AI responses.

---

## 📞 2. Zoom / Google Meet Integration
Fully automated online class links synced with batches.

* **Trigger**: Customer requests a seamless transition between offline/online modes.
* **Key Features**:
  * Auto-generation of meeting links for scheduled batch lectures.
  * One-click join button on student and teacher calendars.
  * Attendance tracking based on participant duration in the virtual meeting room.

---

## 📈 3. Premium Parent App & Custom Branding
Allowing high-end coaching centers to white-label the student portal.

* **Key Features**:
  * Dynamic styling matching the tenant's primary colors, fonts, and subdomains (e.g. `learn.sharmaphysics.com`).
  * Dedicated Android/iOS shell apps compiled with the institute's branding.
